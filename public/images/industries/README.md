# Industry Images

Upload your industry images to this folder with the following naming convention:

## Required Images

1. **oil-gas.jpg** - Oil & Gas industry
2. **industrial-infrastructure.jpg** - Industrial & Infrastructure
3. **information-technology.jpg** - Information Technology
4. **logistics.jpg** - Logistics
5. **healthcare.jpg** - Healthcare
6. **fmcg.jpg** - FMCG (Fast-Moving Consumer Goods)
7. **hospitality.jpg** - Hospitality

## Image Requirements

- **Format**: JPG, PNG, or WebP
- **Recommended Size**: 1200x800px (3:2 aspect ratio)
- **File Size**: Optimize to under 500KB for best performance
- **Quality**: High quality, professional images

## Optional: Additional Industry Images

You can also add images for industries that currently don't have them:
- engineering.jpg
- power.jpg
- automobile.jpg
- telecom.jpg
- financial-services.jpg
- outsourcing-offshoring.jpg
- facility-management.jpg
- media-entertainment.jpg
- metals-minerals-mining.jpg
- services.jpg

To enable an image for an industry, update the `hasImage: true` property in `app/industries/IndustriesPageClient.tsx` and add the image path.
